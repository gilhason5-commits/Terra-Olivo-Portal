Situated at the foot of the Andes in San Juan, Argentina, this prominent producer manages a sprawling 1,000-hectare olive grove. Utilizing state-of-the-art extraction technology, they craft Kosher-certified, globally recognized extra virgin olive oils from Arbequina, Coratina, and Picual varieties. Their commitment to early harvesting, traceability, and sustainable farming ensures exceptional quality for their award-winning products.
