Markakis Vasileios & Sia OE is a respected Greek olive oil press located in Hani Alexandrou, Rethymno, on the island of Crete. They specialize in crafting "Eleou Vios," a premium extra virgin olive oil exclusively derived from the renowned Koroneiki olive variety. Their dedication to authentic Cretan traditions has garnered local and international praise for the quality of their olive oil.
