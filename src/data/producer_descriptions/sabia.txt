Azeite Sabiá is an acclaimed Brazilian producer whose extraordinary extra virgin olive oils consistently rank among the world's finest. Cultivating varieties like Koroneiki and Arbequina in both the Serra da Mantiqueira and Serra do Sudeste regions, founders Bia Pereira and Bob Costa emphasize meticulous cold extraction and sustainable agriculture. Their relentless pursuit of perfection frequently earns them "Best of the Southern Hemisphere" titles at prestigious international contests.
