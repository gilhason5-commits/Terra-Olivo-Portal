Founded in 1872 and currently led by its fifth generation, Aceites García de la Cruz is a historic family-owned producer situated in Madridejos, Toledo, near the foothills of the Montes de Toledo in central Spain. The estate is celebrated for its deep commitment to sustainable agriculture and innovation, crafting award-winning organic extra virgin olive oils. By carefully cultivating traditional varieties like Cornicabra, Picual, Arbequina, and Hojiblanca, they produce exceptionally high-quality, internationally certified oils that honor their rich Spanish heritage.
