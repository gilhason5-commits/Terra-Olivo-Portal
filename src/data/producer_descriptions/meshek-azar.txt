Located in the agricultural community of Sde Moshe, Meshek Azar is a highly regarded Israeli producer of exceptional extra virgin olive oils. Their carefully crafted Picual and signature house blends have garnered international acclaim, including top medals at the TerraOlivo competition. Proudly registered with Israel's "Olive Branch" council, the farm is a testament to the thriving, quality-focused boutique olive oil industry in the region.
