Rooted in the village of Lagkada near Kalamata, Liokareas Olive Oil is a family-owned producer with a rich heritage spanning over five generations. They expertly blend traditional hand-harvesting with modern cold-pressing technology to craft exceptional Koroneiki extra virgin and unique cold-fused flavored oils. Their premium Greek olive oils have gained significant international acclaim, particularly in the United States where they maintain a strong distribution presence.
