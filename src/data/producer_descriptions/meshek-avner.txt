Meshek Avner is a distinguished boutique olive oil producer situated in Beit Itzhak, Israel, celebrated for its premium extra virgin olive oils. Cultivating a diverse range of olive varieties—including Coratina, Koroneiki, and Barnea—the family-run farm consistently earns top honors in prestigious EVOO rankings. Bearing the esteemed "Olive Branch" quality seal, their products represent the highest standards of Israeli olive cultivation and craftsmanship.
