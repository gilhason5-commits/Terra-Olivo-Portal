Meshek Achiya is a prominent Israeli agricultural estate located in the Shiloh area, renowned for its award-winning extra virgin olive oils. Operating an advanced on-site cold-press mill, they expertly extract oil from premium varieties including Barnea, Fishulin, and Arbequina. Their commitment to quality has established them as a leading brand in the Israeli culinary scene, favored by chefs and specialty food purveyors alike.
