Founded in 1956, this cooperative represents over 1,000 families in the Trás-os-Montes region of Portugal. Producing the highly awarded "Azeite Porca de Murça", they expertly combine traditional methods with modern technology to craft exceptional DOP-certified extra virgin olive oils. Benefiting from a unique microclimate, their oils consistently rank among the world's best for their authentic, natural, and healthy profile.
