Levin Farm is a respected family-owned boutique estate situated in Moshav Mazor, specializing in high-quality, cold-pressed extra virgin olive oil. Renowned in the Modi'in region for their commitment to sustainable and traditional farming methods, their award-winning olive oils are Kosher LeMehadrin certified. The farm is also a popular agro-tourism destination, offering visitors immersive tasting tours and insights into the olive cultivation process.
