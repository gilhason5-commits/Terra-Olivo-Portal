Located in Moshav Avihayil near Netanya, Levant Olive Oil is a premium boutique brand crafted by the fourth-generation family-run Armoni Farm. Established in 1932, the farm specializes in producing complex, award-winning blends like 'Green Meadow' and 'Sea Breeze' rather than single-varietal oils. Their high-quality, cold-pressed olive oils reflect the rich, historic traditions of the eastern Mediterranean.
