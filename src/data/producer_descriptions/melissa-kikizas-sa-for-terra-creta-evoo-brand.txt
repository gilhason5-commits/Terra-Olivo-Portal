The Terra Creta brand, under the umbrella of historic Greek food company Melissa-Kikizas SA, represents the pinnacle of Cretan extra virgin olive oil. Operating from state-of-the-art facilities in Kolymvari, Chania, they are celebrated for their unwavering commitment to sustainability, traceability, and the Koroneiki olive variety. Through initiatives like the Terra Creta Olive Academy, they continually empower local farmers while delivering exceptional quality to global markets.
