Roma Olivas Agroindústria Ltda is the visionary Brazilian company behind the internationally recognized "Olive Jungle" brand. Based in Farroupilha, Rio Grande do Sul, they craft premium extra virgin olive oils—including exceptional Koroneiki and artisanal blends—using environmentally sustainable practices. Their commitment to artisanal harvesting and gastronomic excellence has made their oils a standout in global quality and design competitions.
