Located in the rugged Douro Superior region of Portugal, this historic, family-owned estate has been cultivating olives since the 17th century. As pioneers of organic farming, they produce premium extra virgin olive oils like their acclaimed "Grande Escolha", crafted from traditional hand-picked varieties such as Madural and Verdeal. Their oils boast complex, rich, and fruity profiles with a characteristic peppery finish, repeatedly earning top honors globally.
