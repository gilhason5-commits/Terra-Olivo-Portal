Rumeli Zirai ve Gıda Yatırımları A.Ş. is a highly respected Turkish producer operating from the historic region of Eceabat, Çanakkale. Renowned for their "Kirte" brand, they masterfully blend regional Edremit and Memecik olives, often emphasizing traditional early harvests for maximum freshness. Their dedication to preserving authentic Turkish flavors has earned them top medals and accolades at both national and international olive oil competitions.
