Based in Giannitsa, Pella, Lamda Foods S.A. has been producing high-quality Greek extra virgin olive oils and table olives since 2009. Utilizing modern, high-standard bottling facilities paired with traditional agricultural expertise, they process olives sourced from premier regions like Messinia. Their renowned product lines, such as 'Lamda Estate' and 'Oleus', are celebrated for their authentic flavors and premium early harvest qualities.
