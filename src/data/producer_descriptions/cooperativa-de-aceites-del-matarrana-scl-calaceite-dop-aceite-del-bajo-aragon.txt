Based in Calaceite, Teruel, this esteemed Spanish cooperative unifies hundreds of local producers to craft exceptional extra virgin olive oil under the D.O. Bajo Aragón. Specializing in oils made exclusively from 100% Empeltre olives, their products are known for a smooth, sweet profile with subtle almond and fruity notes. They utilize low-temperature milling processes to preserve the maximum quality, color, and flavor of their internationally recognized oils.
