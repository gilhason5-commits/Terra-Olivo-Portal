Based in Havran on the slopes of the Kazdağı mountains, Gökduman Tarım produces premium Turkish extra virgin olive oils under the renowned "Nermin Hanım Zeytinliği" brand. Combining sustainable agriculture with modern cold-pressing techniques, they process high-quality early harvest varieties to preserve maximum antioxidants and polyphenols. Their exceptional oils are consistent gold-medal winners at prestigious international olive oil competitions.
