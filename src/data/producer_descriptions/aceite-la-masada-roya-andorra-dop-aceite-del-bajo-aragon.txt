Located in Andorra within the renowned Bajo Aragón region of Spain, La Masada Roya is a distinguished producer of premium extra virgin olive oil certified under the DOP Aceite del Bajo Aragón. Founded by Pedro Valero, the estate specializes in early harvest, cold-extracted oils crafted from native varieties like the traditional Empeltre. Their meticulously crafted oils are celebrated for their harmonious flavor profiles featuring delicate notes of almond and apple, earning them prestigious international accolades. Combining decades of expertise with modern, sustainable practices, La Masada Roya's exquisite oils reflect a deep dedication to preserving the authentic, vibrant essence of Aragón's terroir.
