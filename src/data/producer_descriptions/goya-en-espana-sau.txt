Operating from Alcalá de Guadaíra in Seville, Goya En España S.A.U. is the esteemed Spanish subsidiary of Goya Foods, dedicated to crafting 100% Spanish extra virgin olive oils. Sourcing exclusively from premier Andalusian olive groves, they oversee rigorous blending and bottling processes to ensure single-country traceability and unblended purity. Renowned for strict quality standards, their award-winning oils frequently rank among the world's most awarded.
