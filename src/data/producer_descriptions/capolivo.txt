Cultivated at Fazenda Tarumã da Boa Vista in Canguçu, Brazil, Capolivo produces premium, award-winning extra virgin olive oil. The Capoani family leverages the region's excellent terroir to grow noble European varieties like Koroneiki, Arbequina, and Frantoio. With a commitment to freshness, low acidity, and careful harvesting, their artisan oils frequently win gold medals at major global competitions.
