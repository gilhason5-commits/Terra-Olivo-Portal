A.M.G. Karabelas P.C., operating as "The Olive Temple," is a fourth-generation, family-run olive oil producer located in Ancient Olympia, Greece. They manage a state-of-the-art mill where olives are processed within three hours of harvest, ensuring the highest quality for their award-winning extra virgin olive oils like Laurel & Flame. The producer stands out for its commitment to traceability through the "MY ID Project," allowing consumers to scan a QR code to access detailed information about the oil's origin and production.
