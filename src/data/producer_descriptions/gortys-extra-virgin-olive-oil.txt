Rooted in the historic Dionisi region of Crete, near the ancient ruins of Gortyna, Gortys is an acclaimed Greek extra virgin olive oil brand run by a three-generation family. They exclusively cultivate organic Koroneiki olives, globally prized for their rich aromatic profile and exceptional health benefits. The brand beautifully intertwines its award-winning organic production with the deep archaeological and cultural heritage of Minoan olive cultivation.
