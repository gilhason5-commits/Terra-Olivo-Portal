Situated in Coronel Dorrego, the provincial capital of the olive in Buenos Aires, Finca La Comarca is a premier Argentine olive oil producer. The estate meticulously oversees the entire production process from its own groves to its on-site mill, crafting highly acclaimed extra virgin olive oils like their award-winning Arbequina. Additionally, the farm is a prominent destination on the regional "Ruta del Olivo," offering immersive oleotourism experiences.
