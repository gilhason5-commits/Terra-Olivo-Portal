LIÁ Cultivators is an acclaimed producer of premium Koroneiki extra virgin olive oil based in Filiatra, Messinia. Merging traditional hand-harvesting techniques with innovative, sustainable production, they extract their exceptional oil early and store it in iconic opaque white bottles. Founded by siblings who operate the LIÁ Olive Oil Hub, their EVOO consistently earns prestigious 'Best-in-Class' awards globally.
