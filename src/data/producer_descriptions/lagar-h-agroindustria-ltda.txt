Located in Cachoeira do Sul, Rio Grande do Sul, Lagar H Agroindustria is a prominent Brazilian producer of premium, fresh extra virgin olive oils. Thriving in a Mediterranean-like climate, the estate focuses on sustainable practices and rapid processing to preserve vital biophenols. Their expertly crafted blends and monovarietals, such as their signature Koroneiki, regularly earn prestigious 'Best of South America' awards at international competitions.
