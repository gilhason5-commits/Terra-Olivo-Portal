Nestled near Sparta in Laconia, Sakellaropoulos Organic Farms is a celebrated family-run estate renowned for its pioneering single-estate organic extra virgin olive oils. Since 1992, they have seamlessly blended traditional farming with scientific innovation to craft award-winning gourmet infused oils and high-polyphenol "bio-functional" varieties. Their unyielding dedication to health and quality makes them one of the most internationally decorated organic olive oil producers globally.
