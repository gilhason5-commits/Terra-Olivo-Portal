Reales Almazaras de Alcañiz is the flagship extra virgin olive oil brand of Cooperativa del Mezquín, located in La Codoñera, Spain. Representing over 450 local growers and 3,000 hectares of groves, the cooperative exclusively uses native Empeltre olives to craft their internationally awarded oils. Certified under the D.O.P. "Aceite del Bajo Aragón," their production perfectly captures the balanced, fruity profile and rich heritage of the Teruel region.
