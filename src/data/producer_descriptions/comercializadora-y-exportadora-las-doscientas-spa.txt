Situated in the fertile Maule Valley near Talca, Chile, Las Doscientas produces high-quality, cold-pressed extra virgin olive oil. Cultivating varieties like Arbequina, Picual, and Frantoio, they prioritize freshness by milling their non-GMO olives within four hours of harvest. Their oils are highly regarded for their rich polyphenol content, balanced flavor profiles, and impressive track record of international awards.
